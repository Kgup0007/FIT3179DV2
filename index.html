Has Boston Gotten Safer?

Boston is a major city in the northeastern United States, with a crime rate below the national average. However, there are still some neighborhoods with higher crime rates than others. The neighborhoods with the highest crime rates in 2022 were Dudley Square, Roxbury, and Dorchester. These neighborhoods account for a disproportionate share of Boston's violent crime. There are a number of factors that contribute to the high crime rates in these neighborhoods, including poverty, unemployment, and lack of access to resources. The city is working to address these issues through a variety of programs and initiatives, but there is still more work to be done.
